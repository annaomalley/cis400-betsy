Betsy uses data from roughly 1900 La Liga games, scraped from fourfourtwo.com, to predict soccer match outcomes.

Using a machine learning model and training it on hundreds of games, we attempt to predict the outcome of the game. This prediction, we hope, will assist people betting on matches earn more money. Our model's success rate is about the same as the Bookies, the people who maintain the betting pool and make a profit off of the results.
